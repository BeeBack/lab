Submit Your Resource

We're excited you’re interested in sharing your resources! Please complete the form and submit it for review. Keep note that we accept and publish content for the categories we currently have available. If you are interested in submitting for a different software, do tell us a little about it in our contact section and we can discuss that way. Furthermore, if you have any questions, you can contact us here.

Before you submit, you will need to:

  • Fill up all required fields.
  • Must include source files that are editable (.sketch, .fw.png, .psd, etc.). It’s best if the items are properly layered and labeled.
  • You will also need to include a preview image for your shared item. We support any image sizes that are at least 800 x 600 px.
  • Compress all your files into a .zip or .rar format and upload it to any cloud platforms. We recommend Dropbox, Google Drive, One Drive and Box. We will manually upload it to our company server.
